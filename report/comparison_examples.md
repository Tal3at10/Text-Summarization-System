# Text Summarization Comparison: Original vs. Summaries

This document presents a side-by-side comparison of the original articles and the summaries generated by our system during the final accuracy test.

## Sample 1: Climate Change Agreement

### Original Text (The Source)
> The US and China have reached a historic agreement on climate change. Both nations committed to significantly reducing carbon emissions by 2030. President Biden stated that this is a critical step for the planet's future. China's President Xi echoed the sentiment, emphasizing global cooperation. The deal includes goals for renewable energy and phasing out coal power plants. Environmental groups have praised the deal while calling for even more aggressive targets.

### Human Reference (The Gold Standard)
> United States and China agree on historic climate deal to reduce emissions by 2030. Presidents Biden and Xi emphasize global cooperation. Deal includes targets for renewable energy and coal phase-out.

### Best System Summary (BART Abstractive)
> Both nations committed to significantly reducing carbon emissions by 2030. The deal includes goals for renewable energy and phasing out coal power plants. Environmental groups have praised the deal while calling for even more aggressive targets. China's President Xi echoed the sentiment, emphasizing global cooperation.

## Sample 2: NASA James Webb Telescope

### Original Text (The Source)
> NASA's James Webb Space Telescope has captured a stunning new image of a distant nebula. The image provides unprecedented detail of star formation in the Pillars of Creation. Astronomers are excited about the new data revealing the chemical composition of cosmic dust. The telescope, launched in 2021, is the most powerful space observatory ever built. It is located 1.5 million kilometers from Earth at the Second Lagrange Point. This discovery helps scientists understand how stars are born and evolve in our galaxy.

### Human Reference (The Gold Standard)
> James Webb Telescope captures detailed image of the Pillars of Creation. Image reveals new data on star formation and cosmic dust. Observatory is located 1.5 million kilometers from Earth.

### Best System Summary (BART Abstractive)
> Image provides unprecedented detail of star formation in the Pillars of Creation. The James Webb Space Telescope is the most powerful space observatory ever built. It is located 1.5 million kilometers from Earth at the Second Lagrange Point. Discovery helps scientists understand how stars are born and evolve in our galaxy.
